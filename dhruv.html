Storage Tips GUIDE

Rice & Wheat
  • ▸ Store in airtight steel or HDPE containers
  • ▸ Add dried neem leaves to deter pests
  • ▸ Keep cool, dry, dark — away from walls
  • ▸ Shelf life: 1–2 years if stored correctly
  • ▸ Freeze small quantities to kill weevil eggs
Dal (Lentils)
  • ▸ Use airtight glass or food-grade plastic jars
  • ▸ Add a pinch of turmeric to repel insects
  • ▸ Keep away from moisture — dals absorb humidity
  • ▸ Shelf life: 2–3 years whole; 1 year split
  • ▸ Do not store near onion/garlic
Cooking Oil
  • ▸ Store in sealed containers away from light
  • ▸ Never store near stove or in direct sunlight
  • ▸ Mustard oil: 18 mo vs refined: 12 mo
  • ▸ Once opened, use within 3–6 months
  • ▸ Check for rancidity smell before use
Potatoes & Onions
  • ▸ Store potatoes in dark, ventilated mesh bags
  • ▸ Keep potatoes & onions SEPARATE
  • ▸ Never refrigerate onions
  • ▸ Check weekly, remove sprouting ones
  • ▸ Potatoes 2–4 wks; onions 1–2 months
Petrol & Diesel
  • ▸ Use approved jerry cans only — no plastic bottles
  • ▸ Store in cool, ventilated area away from sparks
  • ▸ Petrol shelf life: 3–6 months; diesel: 6–12 mo
  • ▸ Add fuel stabiliser if storing 3+ months
  • ▸ Never store inside your home
LPG Cylinders
  • ▸ Store upright in well-ventilated outdoor area
  • ▸ Check valve and regulator seals monthly
  • ▸ Keep away from heat sources & open flames
  • ▸ One 14.2kg cylinder ≈ 45–60 days for 2 adults
  • ▸ Register for emergency refill at local agency
Salt & Sugar
  • ▸ Salt is effectively indefinite shelf life if dry
  • ▸ Sugar can harden — use airtight container with silica
  • ▸ Both are hygroscopic — keep away from steam
  • ▸ Store jaggery in wax paper + airtight box
  • ▸ Label containers with date of storage
General Rules
  • ▸ FIFO: First In, First Out — rotate stock regularly
  • ▸ Inspect monthly; log expiry dates
  • ▸ Keep a printed copy of your stock list offline
  • ▸ Designate one dry, cool corner as your "store"
  • ▸ Keep a small torch & pen near your store room
