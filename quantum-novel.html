The Book

A startup, a machine, and the physics underneath

The second novel follows a trio of founders building a do-it-yourself quantum machine — the kind of build I actually attempt in the lab, written by someone who knows where the wires go. It is about ambition, friendship under pressure, and the line between a computer and the thing it is modelling.

DIY quantum machine quantum computing founder trio garage hardware ambition & trust

"They thought they were building a computer. The computer had other ideas about what counted as an answer."

Plot, characters, and where it lands stay unwritten in public — it isn't finished, and a teaser shouldn't spoil a book that doesn't exist yet. The grounding is real: this story leans on the same quantum builds documented elsewhere in this hub.
