Experiment Mode
Select when the quantum random number is drawn relative to your click. Each mode tests a different aspect of the observer-collapse hypothesis.
Mode 1
POST
QRNG drawn at observe phase start. Outcome fixed before you guess. Tests anti-correlation with a pre-determined result.
QRNG → observe → GUESS → reveal
Mode 2
PRE
You guess first, QRNG drawn after click. Tests whether expectation influences quantum collapse at the moment it occurs.
observe → GUESS → QRNG → reveal
Mode 3
SIMULTANEOUS
QRNG drawn at the exact tick of your click. Conscious decision and quantum event co-occur in the same moment.
observe → [GUESS + QRNG] → reveal
Mode 4
BLIND
QRNG drawn at a random hidden moment during observe or guess phase. Neither you nor the app knows when. Pure baseline.
QRNG at random t → observe → GUESS → reveal
Mode 5
DELAYED
QRNG drawn at observe start, result hidden for 5s after your click before revealing. Tests if revelation moment matters.
QRNG → observe → GUESS → 5s delay → reveal
